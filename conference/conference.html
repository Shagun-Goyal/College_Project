Conference Themes & Tracks

Conference Theme :

The future of innovation: Digital Technologies for Smart world

Track 1

Theme : Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Topics of Interest (but not limited to):

  • Deep Learning Architectures & Applications (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers)
  • Generative AI & Foundation Models (LLMs, Diffusion Models)
  • Reinforcement & Inductive Learning
  • Knowledge Representation & Reasoning
  • Natural Language Processing & Speech Technologies
  • Adaptive & Intelligent Control Systems
  • Explainable AI (XAI), Trustworthy & Responsible AI
  • AI for Healthcare, Education, Agriculture, and Industry 4.0
  • AI for Epidemic and Pandemic Forecasting
  • Cognitive Computing & Neuromorphic AI
  • Robotics & Autonomous Intelligent Agents
  • Swarm Intelligence & Collective AI Systems

Track 2

Theme : Data Science & Analytics

Topics of Interest (but not limited to):

  • Big Data Analytics & Scalable Frameworks (Hadoop, Spark, Flink)
  • Data Acquisition, Cleaning, Transformation & Integration
  • Statistical Modeling, Hypothesis Testing, and Inference
  • Predictive, Prescriptive & Descriptive Analytics
  • Real-Time Data Processing & Stream Analytics
  • Graph & Network Data Science
  • Spatial, Temporal & Spatio-Temporal Analytics
  • Social Media, Web, and Sentiment Analytics
  • Data Visualization & Interactive Dashboards
  • Cloud & Edge-based Data Science Platforms
  • Explainable Analytics & Bias Mitigation
  • Data Privacy, Governance & Ethics in Analytics
  • Applications in Healthcare, Finance, Smart Cities, and Climate Research

Track 3

Theme : Information Technology, IoT & Smart Systems

Topics of Interest (but not limited to):

  • Internet of Things (IoT) Applications in Smart Cities, Healthcare & Industry
  • Cyber-Physical Systems Design, Simulation & Control
  • Smart Homes, Smart Grids & Smart Transportation
  • Edge, Fog & Cloud-Integrated IoT Architectures
  • Blockchain & Distributed Ledger in IoT
  • FinTech Innovations & Digital Payment Systems
  • Adaptive User Interfaces & Human-Computer Interaction
  • Autonomous Vehicles & Drone Technologies
  • Digital Twin Technologies for Industrial IoT
  • Energy-Aware IoT Devices & Networks
  • AR/VR-enabled IoT Applications
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA) in IT & IoT Systems

Track 4

Theme : Cybersecurity & Trust in Intelligent Systems

Topics of Interest (but not limited to):

  • Cybersecurity Policies, Frameworks & Zero-Trust Architectures
  • Cryptography, Cryptanalysis & Post-Quantum Cryptography
  • Secure Software Engineering & Code Auditing
  • Network & Cloud Security, Intrusion Detection Systems
  • IoT & Embedded Systems Security
  • Blockchain Security & Smart Contract Verification
  • Privacy-Preserving AI & Federated Learning Security
  • Digital Forensics, Threat Intelligence & Malware Analysis
  • Cybersecurity for Critical Infrastructure & Smart Cities
  • Human-Centric Cybersecurity & Social Engineering Defenses
  • Trust, Ethics & Accountability in Intelligent Systems

Track 5

Theme : Sustainable & Green Computing

Topics of Interest (but not limited to):

  • Green Data Centers & Energy-Efficient Computing Architectures
  • Sustainable Software Engineering Practices
  • AI for Climate Change Mitigation & Environmental Monitoring
  • Renewable Energy Forecasting & Optimization with AI/IoT
  • Low-Power IoT Devices & Energy-Aware Networks
  • Smart Grids, Microgrids & Energy Management Systems
  • Sustainable Cloud, Fog & Edge Computing
  • E-Waste Reduction & Circular Economy Models in IT
  • Carbon-Neutral Computing & Green Blockchain Solutions
  • Computing for UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Track 6

Theme : Emerging Technologies & Next-Generation Intelligent Systems

Topics of Interest (but not limited to):

  • Quantum Computing: Algorithms, Cryptography & Applications
  • Neuromorphic & Brain-Inspired Computing Models
  • Digital Twins & Metaverse Applications for Industry & Education
  • Generative AI for Creativity, Design & Simulation
  • Human-Centered Computing & Adaptive Interfaces
  • Autonomous & Self-Evolving Systems
  • AR/VR/MR Applications in Healthcare, Education & Industry 5.0
  • 5G/6G Networks & Ultra-Low Latency Applications
  • Bio-Inspired & DNA Computing Approaches
  • Swarm Intelligence & Self-Organizing Systems
  • Space Computing & Interplanetary Communication Systems
